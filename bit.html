Bannari ammam instution of technology

Bannari Amman Institute of Technology (BAIT) was established by the Bannari Amman Group in the year 1996 . BAIT is affiliated to Anna University and is situated in the Erode area of Tamil Nadu. Bannari Amman Institute of Technology (BAIT) offers 15 Undergraduate and 15 Postgraduate courses in Engineering and Management disciplines. BAIT runs the Ph.D. program under its Engineering and Technology office which is perceived by the Anna University, Chennai. Admission to UG program is given based on Tamil Nadu Engineering Admission (TNEA) score in light of XII standard qualifying examination result. Though admission to the PG program is given based on TANCET led by the Anna University.

Application form of TNEA will be accessible online just between the first week of May to the fourth week of May. The application form for TANCET, then again, is available from the fourth week of January to the fourth week of February. Admission procedure begins in the month of May. Admission is given based on rank got in the qualifying entrance test. Admission to BE/B.Tech is given through Unified Single Window Admission System and ME/M.Tech is given based on entrance test taken after by counseling.

Applicants looking for admission in BE/B.Tech must have passed HSC or proportional qualifying examination with the average percentage of marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Admission to ME/M.Tech is given based on merit list of TANCET and inclination will be given to the GATE qualified candidates. According to the rule and policies of Anna University, reservation is given to the applicants having a place with SC/ST/OBC/SCA/Mos t Backward Class and DNC/Backward Muslim classifications.
